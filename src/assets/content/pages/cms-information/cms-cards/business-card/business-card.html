The Business Cards is a self-contained Angular component designed to display professional contact details in a sleek, responsive layout. It features a large circular profile picture positioned in the top-left corner, with the name, contact details, and social media links neatly arranged beside it. A bio section is centered at the bottom, ensuring a balanced design. Built with Bootstrap and SASS, it supports three cards per row on larger screens while stacking on smaller devices. Ideal for company websites, freelancer portfolios, networking events, and customer support teams, this component offers a visually appealing and reusable way to showcase professionals.
